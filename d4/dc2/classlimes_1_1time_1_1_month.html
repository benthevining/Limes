Detailed Description

This class represents a month of the Gregorian calendar.

The months are numbered like so:

Number Name
1 January
2 February
3 March
4 April
5 May
6 June
7 July
8 August
9 September
10 October
11 November
12 December
See also
Date, DateTime
Test:
This class's API is tested with static assertions at compile-time.
